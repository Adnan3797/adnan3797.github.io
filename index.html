Adnan Hussain

I am currently pursuing a PhD specializing in Computer Vision, Pattern Recognition, and Multimedia Data Analysis, with a strong background in Deep Learning and Intelligent Systems. I serve as the Coordinator of the Intelligent Media Laboratory (IMLab), where I have led a research group of seven members and managed multiple interdisciplinary research projects. My primary research interests include video anomaly detection and recognition using advanced deep learning models. I also work in related areas such as person re-identification, object detection, population projection, and battery health prediction. I have applied advanced deep learning techniques including active learning, incremental learning, and adaptive model design to build scalable and intelligent solutions. Additionally, I integrate Explainable AI (XAI) approaches to ensure transparency, reliability, and interpretability of AI models.
